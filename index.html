Our Story

Built By A Family,
For Your Family.

Fred T. Williams started Williams Electric in 1957. A Master Electrician — and Master Plumber — he built homes from the ground up and loved nothing more than a complicated old remodel. He laid the foundation for everything we are today.

His son, Tim Williams, earned his Colorado Journeyman license in 1983 and reborn the business as Williams & Sons Electric in 1988. Since 1992 Tim has carried his dad's original Contractor License #583 — one of Denver's oldest still active — along with his Master and Journeyman licenses. With over 30 years of experience and the third generation now learning the trade, the family name still stands behind every job.
